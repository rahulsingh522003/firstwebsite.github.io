Courses which we offer

Ram Lal College of engineering(RLCOE) is a well known college for the courses which it offer.

Bachelor of Technology (BTech)

Bachelor of Technology (BTech) is a professional undergraduate engineering degree programme awarded to candidates after the completion of four years of study in the field.

Master of Technology (MTech)

Master of Technology (MTech) is a professional engineering degree programme awarded to candidates which has done their BTech in any field.This course is of two year.

Integrated Dual Degree(IDD)

This course is of five year which includes both BTech and MTech.This can be in any feild and the best part is that it reduces the time of graduation.
